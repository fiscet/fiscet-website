Apix CRM

Description

Verticalized CRM for the management of customers and sales in the mediation of electronic components. The central part of the program is based on customers, from which other managements branch off such as the registry of customer managers, contacts and interactions with customers, sales details divided by principal, annual final balances, etc.

All lists are pageable, filterable and selectable. To access the details, just double click on the line, while to access other details there are special links on the entity (for example, the product sheet from the sales list).

All entities are manageable either from the main menu or from the settings.

Also there is a section for importing Customers, Products and Sales from a csv file

Stack

Backend

  • RESTful server in CodeIgniter 4
  • MariaDB
  • JWT authentication

Frontend

  • ReactJs 16 with Typescript
  • Material UI 4
  • Material XGrid Pro
  • React-query with Axios
  • React-i18 (the app is multilanguage)
  • Recoil state management
